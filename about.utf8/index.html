About Me

January 1, 0001

![a pic](Plane/IMG_4763.jpg) - __Name:__ Dzhaner Emin - __Ocupation:__ PhD Student - __Institution:__ [Instute for Bio- and Geoscience](http://www.fz-juelich.de/portal/EN/institutes/InstituteBioGeosciences/_node.html), Research Center Jülich, Germany - __Hobbies:__ Birdwatching, Weighlifting, Cycling .

Dzhaner Sami Emin studied Ecology and environmental science at theForestry University of Sofia, where he expressed interest in forestry management, nature conservation, spatial analysis, and remote sensing. He specialized in Geo-information Science and Remote Sensing for Environmental Modeling and Management at the Geography department of Lund University (Sweden) and at the Institute for Geo-information Science and Earth Observation of Twente Unverisity (The Netherlands). He is pursuing a PhD in Remote Sensing of Vegetation at the Research Institute for Bio- and Geoscience of Jülich.

Dzhaner has worked as Remote Sensing Specialist at EFTAS Fernerkundung, as independent expert on mapping of conservation areas. During his tenure at Jülich Research Institute he has tutored Master and Bachelor students.

He has extensive experience in planning and excecuting field activities in agriculutural and natural areas. Familiar with specifics of survey methodologies. Working with field hyperspectral devices and other environmental equipment. Proficient in identification of plant and animal species composition. Data organisiation and analysis.
